Before You Trade

Everything a new trader needs to get started safely on RISEx ‚Äî from wallet setup to risk management.

Step 01

Set Up Your Wallet

RISEx is an EVM-compatible dApp. You'll need an Ethereum-compatible wallet connected to RISE Chain. Use the RISE Wallet (passkey + gasless) or MetaMask.

Step 02

Understand Perpetuals

Perpetual futures (perps) are derivatives that let you go long or short on an asset's price without expiry. Key concepts every trader must know:

  • Funding rate ‚Äî paid between longs and shorts periodically
  • Mark price vs index price ‚Äî used for liquidation calculations
  • Leverage ‚Äî amplifies both gains and losses
  • Liquidation ‚Äî your position closes if collateral hits the threshold
Step 03

Choose Your Margin Mode

RISEx offers portfolio margin ‚Äî allowing your entire portfolio to back all open positions. This is more capital efficient but increases systemic risk exposure.

  • Portfolio margin ‚Äî cross-collateral, capital efficient
  • Multiple supported assets as collateral
  • AutoYield earns on idle collateral automatically
Step 04

Risk Management Essentials

High leverage trading carries extreme risk. Never trade more than you can afford to lose. Recommended practices for new traders:

  • Start with 2‚Äì5x leverage to learn the platform
  • Always set mental stop-loss levels before entering
  • Monitor funding rates ‚Äî they impact profitability
  • Keep 20‚Äì30% of collateral as buffer above liquidation
Step 05

Unlike CEX perpetuals, everything on RISEx is verifiable. This changes how trust works in your favor:

Quick Reference
Trader's Glossary

Key terms every RISEx trader should understand.

Perpetual Future (Perp)
A derivative contract with no expiry date. You can hold a long or short position indefinitely, subject to funding rates.
Funding Rate
A periodic payment between long and short holders to keep the perp price anchored to the spot market. Positive = longs pay shorts.
Mark Price
A fair value price derived from index and funding data, used to calculate unrealized PnL and trigger liquidations. Not the last trade price.
Leverage
Multiplier applied to your collateral. 10x leverage means a 1% move in the asset moves your position by 10%. Increases risk proportionally.
Liquidation
Forced closure of a position when your collateral falls below the maintenance margin threshold. The liquidation engine is transparent and onchain.
Portfolio Margin
Margin mode where your entire portfolio's net value backs all positions. More capital efficient ‚Äî but losses anywhere affect all positions.
AutoYield
RISEx's feature that automatically earns yield on idle collateral by routing it through onchain yield strategies. Active while you hold positions.
Sub-Account
A programmable child account under your main account. Use it to isolate strategies, run automated bots, or build custom trading primitives.
Composability
The ability to interact with multiple DeFi protocols (lending, AMMs, vaults) atomically in a single transaction ‚Äî unique to onchain execution.
